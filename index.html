Supported Micro/Nanofabrication Capabilities

We facilitate access to advanced R&D and pilot/volume production lines (including 6-inch R&D and 8-inch pilot lines). Our network supports a comprehensive range of process packages:

Core Facility & Process Coverage

  • **Line Access:** 6-inch R&D lines and 8-inch pilot/volume production lines.
  • **Epitaxy (External Access):** GaN, InP, GaAs, and support for Si/Sapphire/etc. substrates.
  • **Core Processes:** Lithography (Double-side, Stepper, E-beam), Etching (RIE, ICP, IBE, DRIE, VHF, Trimming), CVD, PVD (Sputtering, E-beam, Thermal, PLD), Ion Implantation.

Compound Semiconductors & Optoelectronics

  • **GaN HEMT:** Full-flow process for D-mode and E-mode devices, supporting 2", 4", 6", and 8" wafers, including monolithic DCFL/CMOS integration.
  • **Micro-LED Displays:** Access to 8" Micro-LED process lines, covering full flow and wafer-level monolithic integration of pixels and drivers.
  • **III-V Devices:** Fabrication and packaging support for various III-V optoelectronic devices.

Advanced MEMS and Microsystems

  • **Piezoelectric MEMS (AlScN/PZT):** Process for high-performance (002) AlScN (with ion milling) and PZT (including (110) preferred). Applications include FBAR filters, ultrasonic transducers, and gyroscopes.
  • **Acoustic Devices:** Bulk/Surface Acoustic Wave Resonators (Filters).
  • **Special Structures:** Wafer bonding (Si-Glass, Au-In, Au-Sn, etc.) and release cavity structures (Front/Back/Sealed).
  • **Quantum Devices:** MEMS-based Atomic Vapor Cell technology for chip-level atomic clocks, gyroscopes, and magnetometers.

Integration and Specialized Processes

  • **Heterogeneous Integration:** TSV/TGV (Through-Silicon/Glass Via) with high aspect ratio electroplating, Chiplet technology, horizontal array splicing, and advanced bonding (Anodic/Eutectic/Hybrid).
  • **Magnetic Devices:** Magnetic Tunnel Junction (MTJ) processing using double-layer photoresist stripping for storage, sensors, and spintronic devices.
  • **Packaging:** Flip-chip bonding, wire bonding, dispensing, thinning, CMP, dicing, and integrated packaging solutions.
