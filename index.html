Supported Micro/Nanofabrication Capabilities (Facility Access)

We facilitate access to advanced R&D and pilot/volume production lines (including 6-inch R&D and 8-inch pilot lines). Our network supports a comprehensive range of process packages and equipment:

Lithography

  • **Equipment:** Double-side Contact Aligner, Stepper Projection System.
  • **Advanced:** Electron Beam Lithography (E-beam).
  • **Application:** Patterning for MEMS, III-V devices, and specialized structures.

Deposition

  • **PVD:** Magnetron Sputtering, E-beam Evaporation, Thermal Evaporation, Pulsed Laser Deposition (PLD).
  • **CVD:** PECVD, ICPCVD, LPCVD, MPCVD.
  • **Special:** Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) support for precision film growth.

Plasma Etching

  • **Equipment:** RIE, ICP, NLD-ICP.
  • **Precision:** Ion Beam Etching (IBE), Trimming.
  • **Deep Etching:** Deep RIE (DRIE) for high aspect ratio structures, VHF (Very High Frequency) etching.

Assembly & Packaging

  • **Wafer Level:** Wafer Bonding (Si-Glass, Au-In, Au-Sn, etc.), TSV/TGV (high aspect ratio).
  • **Chip Level:** Flip-chip bonding, Die Attach, Wire Bonding, Dispensing.
  • **Finishing:** Thinning, CMP, Dicing, Integrated Packaging Solutions.

Analytics & Metrology

  • **Thickness/Profile:** Profilometer, Film Thickness Measurement.
  • **Material/Structure:** X-ray, AFM, Ultrasonic Testing.
  • **Electrical/Mechanical:** I-V/C-V testing, Tensile Testing, Electrical characterization.

Wet Chemistry & Substrate Prep

  • **Epitaxy Support:** Access to GaN, InP, GaAs epitaxial growth, and support for Si/Sapphire/Glass substrates.
  • **Wet Processes:** Standard acid/solvent wet benches for etching, cleaning, and lift-off processes.
  • **Specialized Etching:** Wet chemical etching for specific material removal (e.g., Silicon, Oxide).

Advanced Applications

  • **Piezoelectric MEMS:** (002) preferred AlScN processing (with ion milling) and PZT.
  • **Compound Semi:** GaN HEMT (D/E-mode, DCFL/CMOS), Micro-LED (8" monolithic integration).
  • **Integration:** Chiplet technology, Heterogeneous Integration.
  • **Quantum:** MEMS-based Atomic Vapor Cell (Rb, Cs, buffer gas filling).
